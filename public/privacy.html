Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ArtRig ("we", "us", or "our") operates the ArtRig website. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Affiliate Disclosure: ArtRig participates in the Amazon Associates Program and other affiliate programs. We earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through links on this site, at no extra cost to you.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate the service:

  • Search queries: When you use the AI Finder, your search query is sent to our server to generate recommendations. We may log queries to improve the service.
  • Click data: We track clicks on affiliate links (product name, timestamp) to understand which recommendations are useful. No personal information is linked to these clicks.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs including IP address, browser type, and pages visited. These are retained for up to 30 days.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered product recommendations based on your search query
  • To improve the quality and relevance of our recommendations
  • To track affiliate link performance for revenue reporting
  • To diagnose technical issues

Third-Party Services

ArtRig uses the following third-party services:

  • Tavily Search API: Your search queries are sent to Tavily to retrieve web results. See Tavily's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude AI: Search results are processed by Claude AI to generate recommendations. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Associates: We participate in the Amazon affiliate program. Amazon may use cookies to track purchases originating from our links. See Amazon's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

ArtRig itself does not set cookies. However, affiliate partners (such as Amazon) may set cookies when you click through to their sites. These cookies are used to attribute purchases for commission purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share search queries or click data with third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the service (i.e., sending queries to Tavily and Claude for processing).

Data Retention

Search query logs are retained for up to 90 days. Aggregated, anonymized click data may be retained indefinitely for business analytics.

Children's Privacy

ArtRig is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@artrig.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@artrig.com.
