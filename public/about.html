About Us

Built for Digital Artists,
By a Digital Artist

ArtRig exists because finding the right gear as a digital artist is genuinely hard. Review sites are full of sponsored fluff. Search engines give you a thousand options with no real guidance. And asking in forums means waiting for someone with the same setup as you to show up.

We built ArtRig to cut through that noise. You describe what you need — your budget, your software, your workflow — and our AI searches the web in real time to find the best options, ranked from budget to premium with clear explanations.

Our mission: make it effortless for every digital artist — beginner or professional — to find the right tablet, monitor, GPU, course, or accessory for their setup.

What We Cover

ArtRig covers the full range of gear and learning resources a digital artist needs:

  • ✓ Drawing tablets (Wacom, Huion, XP-Pen and more)
  • ✓ Color-accurate monitors and displays
  • ✓ GPUs optimized for Blender, ZBrush, Unreal Engine, and more
  • ✓ Complete PC builds for 3D animation and rendering
  • ✓ Online courses for Blender, ZBrush, Procreate, Substance, and more
  • ✓ Desk accessories, stands, styluses, and peripherals

How Recommendations Work

Every recommendation on ArtRig is generated in real time. When you search, we use the Tavily web search API to pull current product data, reviews, and pricing from across the web. Then Claude AI analyzes those results and ranks them based on your specific use case — not generic bestseller lists.

We don't maintain a static product database that goes stale. Prices and availability change constantly, so we always pull live data.
